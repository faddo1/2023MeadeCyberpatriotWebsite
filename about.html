What is CyberPatriot?

CyberPatriot is the National Youth Cyber Defense Competition, which puts teams of high school and middle school students in the position of newly hired IT professionals tasked with managing the network of a small company. Through a series of online competition rounds, teams are given a set of virtual operating systems and are tasked with finding and fixing cybersecurity vulnerabilities while maintaining critical services. This is Meade High School's branch. Learn more at the Official Cyberpatriot Website.

How do I join?

There is no sign up sheet or anything. Just pop into the Cyber Lab every Wednesday after school from 2:30-3:30 to participate in the club and its activities. If you are looking to participate in the competitions, it is encouraged that you join as soon as possible so that we can get to know you and your schedule a little more to fit the activities. You can also contact the clubs organizers to help you find the club and join. If you are ready the click the button below!

How can a student benefit?

Win or lose, there is always an opportunity for every student. You can gain internships into big companies, scholarships to some of the best colleges in the country, and national recognition from big names.
